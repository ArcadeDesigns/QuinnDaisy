Reminders of Him by Colleen Hoover

Reminders of Him by Colleen Hoover

The reality behind Love is cute and quite exhausting at the same time. What comes with Love is the complexity of acknowledging the vulnerability that this brings. Unfortunately we don’t choose who we fall in Love with and what’s more is the secret that could come along with it. This book is a very great read; the Reminders of Him by Colleen Hoover is wrapped in a complex plot and setting intended to bring out the best with the use of suspense and a tragic flow. The plot thickens as the read goes on with a complex character and the portray of feminism and control. In a bid to capture reader’s attention the novel makes good use of an Aside and hides details which slowly unravels along with its plot.

It entails a very complex plot and a story meant to make readers stick to my phones and reading gadgets or even the book itself. Colleen Hoover a bestselling author truly puts into good use every writing skill stored up sleeve and released a very good classic, the right mix of tragedy, romance, and suspense with a great plot to support it flow.

In this post, well discuss a bit about the novel without giving too much details or spoilers. The story starts with various details leading up to the Bar where Love is made. There is something unique about bar tenders and the way they move, pulling tricks on innocent customers and unsuspecting college girls on the summers holiday in the city, what’s ironic is; every bar tender has their moment of attraction, masters of tricks and flattery being drew into attractions themselves, that point where the room filled with drinks, drunks and repentant drunks stops receiving focus for a moment and then the quiet, sad looking beauty the very definition of a “Damsel In Distress” commands the attention and sits lonely writing in a diary and read memories from her past. Understand this, Love finds everyone in due time when they least expect it. However all Love story could something have a past, one so twisted and tragic, like the saying goes “its need to know” and anyone expecting gold could be sent off to an Heart break, imagine this, falling in Love with an ex-con, and what’s more complicated is when the pass mixed with tragedy connects two Lovers, and then the secrets begins to threaten the future, with a Murder by the corner, refusing to leave and refusing to stay.

Without giving too much details or any spoilers, experience tragic Love, unlike the story of Othello, or Romeo and Juliet, this story finds its Desdemona guilty of murder and its prince charming caught up in the mist of the whole affair. Simply put, Prince Charming knows the Murder victim and they might be more than friends, a part of the past she hoped was lost and buried in shreds of mysteries.
