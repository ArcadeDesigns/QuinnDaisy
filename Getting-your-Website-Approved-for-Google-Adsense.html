Getting your Website Approved for Google Adsense

CCPA and GPRA policies

When it comes to website policies concerning the usage and collection of data, there are certain policies to put in mind, ranging from the Terms of Usage and Services to Cookie Policies, Privacy Policies, or Disclaimer post, all this details are necessary and very important. In line with the monetization of content, specifically in relation to Google Adsense approval, these policies are very crucial. Various bloggers and Website mangers have resulted to the use of various advert agencies and organizations other than Google Adsense to ensure monetization their post or web content in hopes to earn from traffic and clicks as well as impression, this agencies ranges from each other, to mention a few, Propeller Ads, Adsterra, or even Google Adsense. All this agencies are used in the process of monetization of websites and blog contents however their rates and method of applications differs from each other. For example, Propeller Ads offers a wide range of services however are prone to showing adverts of X rated contents, while Adsterra, dishes out rate which are incredibly low in remuneration, this leaves most bloggers and website managers with Google Adsense which requires tedious processes to get approved for Ads viewing on various websites.

Google Adsense is known to give out extremely high rate on clicks, impressions and traffic. The rates generated by Google Adsense are the highest in the service of Ads placement on websites and blogs; for every 1000 clicks $4.5 would be added to the account, if this is calculated by 100,000 clicks that would bring in $405 with a monthly payout clause, the more reasons to generate organic traffic and avoid plagiarism of contents and images as plagiarism and fake clicks are major reason for the rejection or suspension of a website in the Adsense program. The process of Google Adsense approval required for admission into the Adsense program ranges from the following requirements that must have been met by the website before application. The requirement includes: a minimum of 20 posts on the website and this post must be 100% authentic and free of plagiarism, at least three month of operations for the website to be approved, and the most important are the Privacy Policies.

When it comes to privacy policies, Google Adsense doesn’t joke with this, the reason not far fetch though. Most websites would require its audience or users to enter certain personal details into web forms or subscription boxes, information’s like passwords, gender, account number, card details and more are inputted into this websites which are uploaded to the data base and could be sold off upon extraction, or used in the process of massive bank and financial fraudulent transactions. Either way, guaranteeing the safety of your Audience and Users is very important as this would prevent the occurrence of massive bank and financial fraudulent.

In a bid to get Google Adsense approval, as much as the contents published and the Management of the websites are important and compulsory, also the assurance of users privacy and security of their personal details is also vehement as Google Adsense won’t approve a website with plagiarism or fake traffic and most important without the Privacy Policy notifications or arrangements. The privacy policies are so important, even certain Ads agencies and affiliate marketing platform won’t admit websites or blogs without this assurance. In the case of Google Adsense, beyond the Privacy Policies, Terms of Service and Disclaimer post, there are two Privacy Policies that should be added in the privacy policy section, GPRA policies for users from the EU and the California Privacy Policy quote (CCPA and GPRA policies). The CCPA (California Privacy Policy) is in line with the California Law, which protects residence of California from illegal activities and falling victims to illegal websites. This policies are used to make websites and blogs liable to law actions if found in contempt of this laws and ensure its users and residence protection. It also helps the Adsense program to ascertain serious Websites from the fake ones seeking to gather users information.

Conclusively, if you are hoping to start a website or blog, where your contents would be monetirized for the traffic and impressions generate, then you must certainly put this in mind, and carry out all the necessary research needed to understand this policies, write one and upload it to your Privacy Policy on your website or blog, to guarantee users from the EU and California of the security of their privacy.
