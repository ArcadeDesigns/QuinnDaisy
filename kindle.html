Imagine this, you are an author from Nigeria and you hope to Publish heartfelt written contents about African Culture and the ills of bad governance; moving further, already I presume you’d have your contents set and ready, all you need to do is open an Amazon Kindle Account, register and fill in all the necessary forms and blanks, proceed to publications, create a cover image and you can get your book published either as an Ebook, or Paperback or Hardcover simples. Amazon Kindle and the Process of Publication

The processes involve in the publication of books by Authors on Amazon Kindle are not quite difficult to understand, the very first thing is the presence of a manuscript written and save in docx format, however this does not negate files save an doc or PDF formats, but would be preferable if saved as doc or docx to allow for easy editing and correction of mistakes, as well as set the right margin and word alignment. The next step is to register an account, make all the necessary verification and add the account details and tax payment details as well. After completion of the verification process, then prospective authors and writer can proceed to login and head to the book shelf section of the account created where they’ll find everything required in the process of publishing books on Amazon Kindle. Also let me state that there are various login options on Amazon as a whole, you can create a login for Amazon online store, or for Amazon Kindle or even Amazon affiliate marketing, either way it is important to know which is intended to be used and in the post, we opt for Kindle.

After creating a Kindle account and surfing to the book shelf section of Amazon Kindle page, the rest are self explanatory until it gets to the cover paper or the design for the book cover. At this point a computer system should suffice to do the rest. Amazon Kindle as on its cover photo section some certain template which could be used in the design of the Cover of your book however it s best advised to make use of self designed cover photos for the book, and to get the full gist on this part, a computer system should be used; mainly to get access to the full widgets and tools needed to create an attractive cover back or photo of the book to be published. With all this established, you’d move to the next section which would require you to set a price for the book to be sold, and you can as well make use of a standard price to be sold across borders in various countries like Japan, Canada, the United States, and even the EU, getting this done is the final step in the process of publication and after submission, a review is done to ascertain the book supports Amazon Kindle policies and is Plagiarism free.

Amazon Kindle Prospect of Generating Financial Income

After publication of a book on the kindle platform, with its price set, imagine having a book sales of ten copies for $20 or $45, and a royalty of $15 for each sales, that is an income of $150 from sales, then imagine bigger sales of ten thousand copies or more (10,000) quite a great prospect if you ask me, what is even more is the ability to make sales across borders and spread cultural and socio-political ideologies still across border. Also you might want to take note; when setting the price of your book on Amazon Kindle; the payout price is the Royalty set during the process of the book publication and not the original price set, as that would cover the publication bill and custom duties if there is any.

Adding account details also takes a different process, as this would require setting up an international bank detail, which can be done if you are an author from Africa via the Payoneer platform; and like previous steps taken, registration, and verification of identity and generation of a checking account, and you all set and game.
